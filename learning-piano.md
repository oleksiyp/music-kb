# Learning Piano

You don't need to "learn piano" in the classical sense. Your target is functional fluency in playing electronic music ‚Äì to express musical ideas quickly and play with gear. That's very achievable in 1‚Äì2 years of light, focused practice.

## üéπ Why Piano Helps in Electronic Music

- **Chord fluency** - Laying harmonic foundations of tracks
- **Left-hand bass** - Writing basslines and subs
- **Scale improvisation** - Writing melodies, pads, arps
- **Ear training** - Building musical intuition
- **Rhythm skills** - Better grooves and timing when jamming or sequencing

**Resource:** [Syntorial](https://www.syntorial.com/)

## How to Learn Piano Fast

- Do zero mistakes
- Start without time
- Slow metronome
- Don't increase speed long time till fully ready 
- Fix mistake immediately
- Stop when mistake happened
- Sleep well to memorize
- Practice every day 10-15 mins (min 5 mins)
- Choose optimal complexity, speed

**Video Guide:** [How to Learn Piano Fast](https://youtu.be/VZDmmAhQobY?si=620_a6PVS9c9ykII)

### Categories to Learn

- Reading sheet music
- Scales
- Chords
- Music theory
- Aural skills (listening)

### Common Problems to Avoid

- Tense hands
- Sitting too high/too low
- Using weird fingers
- Looking at hands (not reading music)
- Covering mistakes with sustain
- Don't use stickers

**Video Guide:** [Common Piano Problems](https://youtu.be/p00zsi71t6I?si=CoZwOZ_ROrbEIXET)

### Proper Hand Position

- Don't bend wrist
- Don't put it down
- Relaxed curved position
- Hold enough deep enough into keys

**Video Guide:** [Proper Hand Position](https://youtu.be/U4mU0uu-0Aw?si=yOH3WRF9iCTR0qNI)

### 6 Concepts Beginners Must Know To Learn Fast 

Beginners often fail because they don‚Äôt master six essential piano concepts in order:

1. Pattern Recognition: See chords, not individual notes‚Äîlike recognizing whole words instead of letters.

2. Melody by Ear: Use simple numeric patterns (e.g., 1-2-3) to learn melody quickly; start with three notes and expand.

3. Chord + Melody Integration: Combine chord recognition and melody by ear to learn full songs fast.

4. Weak Pinky Fix: It's not a strength issue‚Äîuse "over-under" wrist rotation for proper technique.

5. 3S Rule: Practice Slow, Separate hands, Small sections to avoid drilling in mistakes.

6. Focus on Gold Chords: Master just six common chords used in 84% of songs‚Äîskip the rest.

Reuse knowledge: Many popular songs share the same four-chord progression‚Äîlearn one, learn many.

Copy-paste chord structures across songs to accelerate learning.

Use a step-by-step system to master songs in weeks instead of years.
